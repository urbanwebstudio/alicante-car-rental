Rental Conditions

Everything you need to know
before you book.

🪪
Age Requirement

Minimum age is 21 years. Drivers aged 21–24 may incur a young driver surcharge of €8/day. No upper age limit.

🚗
Driving Licence

Valid driving licence held for a minimum of 1 year required. EU licences accepted directly. Non-EU licences require an International Driving Permit.

💳
Deposit

A security deposit of €250–€600 (depending on car class) is held on a credit card at pickup. Released within 7 days of return with no damage.

🛡️
Insurance Included

Third-party liability (RC) and basic collision (CDW) are included in all rates. Full coverage with zero excess is available as an add-on.

⚠️
Damage Liability

With standard insurance, you are responsible for damage up to the excess amount (varies by category). Full CDW eliminates this liability entirely.

⛽
Fuel Policy

Full-to-full policy. Vehicle is provided with a full tank and must be returned full. Refuelling charges apply otherwise.
